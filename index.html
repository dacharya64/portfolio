I make games and interactive media through
writing, art, and code.

Storytelling

I write interactive fiction—stories that grow and evolve. Play who you want and do what you will—just remember that your actions have consequences.

Visuals

From 2D art to 3D models, motion graphics to short films, I create content that stands out. My aim is to catch the eye and keep the attention.

Interactivity

I create work that reacts to users, from websites to apps to games. I seek to give people experiences that they want to have—and so far, it's worked.

sub-Q Magazine

Stories that get under your skin.

A literary magazine for interactive fiction—stories where you become the author of your destiny. We publish the strange and the powerful, from established IF authors and those breaking into the genre.

Red Survivor

Good luck, comrade.

Red Survivor, brought to you by Mustachio Games, puts you in control of survivors in war-torn Russia pitted against a murderous robotic threat. Manage your resources. Survive for as long as you can.

Utherous

Explore a living world.

Utherous is a Skill Check Games MMO where the world bends to your will. Be who you truly want to be while building an empire around you. Our Kickstarter is coming soon to a browser near you.

To Those Left Behind

Do you dare defy the gods?

To Those Left Behind combines the the best elements of tabletop RPG and interactive fiction, allowing you to both create and explore the world—and eventually to forge the fate of the nation.
